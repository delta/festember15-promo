Every year without fail there are going to be 4 days of chaos, joy and unparalleled fun. Every year as Festember comes and goes there are highs and lows but in the end it is always about the show. Festember has its roots in the need for engineering students to head bang for something apart from unsolvable equations. It has now evolved into a fest that provides different forms of entertainment for all. This fest that happens every year in September is indeed something to remember.
